[English]
I pledge allegiance to the flag of the United States of America, and to the republic for which it stands, one nation under God, indivisible, with liberty and justice for all.

[toki pona]
mi kepeken e kon len pi ma Mewika en wile wile kulupu lawa. ona li ma wan pi jan sewi. ona li ma wan pi lawa nimi ken en lawa nimi pona tawa jan ale.

[translated back to English]
I am with air-cloth (flag) of America and want-want-group-lead (republic). it is one country with man-above (god). it is one country of lead-word-can (liberty) and lead-word-good (justice) for all people.
